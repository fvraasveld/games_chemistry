## 🎯 HOW TO PLAY:

1. **Look at the 3D Earth** - it's rotating!
2. **Click "Answer Quiz"** - test your chemistry knowledge
3. **Get elements** when you answer correctly
4. **Unlock creatures** - they appear in 3D!
5. **Click "Next Era"** - watch Earth transform
6. **Drag to rotate** the camera view
7. **Scroll to zoom** in and out
